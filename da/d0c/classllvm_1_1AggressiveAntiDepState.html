std::vector<unsigned> llvm::AggressiveAntiDepState::GroupNodes
private

GroupNodes - Implements a disjoint-union data structure to form register groups. A node is represented by an index into the vector. A node can "point to" itself to indicate that it is the parent of a group, or point to another node to indicate that it is a member of the same group as that node.
