unsigned llvm::CGIOperandList::OperandInfo::MIOperandNo

MIOperandNo - Currently (this is meant to be phased out), some logical operands correspond to multiple MachineInstr operands. In the X86 target for example, one address operand is represented as 4 MachineOperands. Because of this, the operand number in the OperandList may not match the MachineInstr operand num. Until it does, this contains the MI operand index of this operand.
