Detailed Description

We have two kinds of SchedReadWrites. Explicitly defined and inferred sequences. TheDef is nonnull for explicit SchedWrites, but Sequence may or may not be empty. TheDef is null for inferred sequences, and Sequence must be nonempty.

IsVariadic controls whether the variants are expanded into multiple operands or a sequence of writes on one operand.
